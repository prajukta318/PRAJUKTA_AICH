Get to Know Us

Oak & Espresso was built on a simple idea — coffee should be honest, flavorful,
and worth taking time for. We source high-quality beans from trusted farms,
roast them in small batches, and serve them with the care you can taste in each sip.


The “Oak” represents our foundation — strong values, long-lasting relationships,
and a commitment to doing things right. The “Espresso” reflects our energy — bold flavors,
vibrant communities, and a love for the craft.


From the first aroma of freshly ground beans to the last sip of a perfectly brewed cup,
we aim to make coffee an experience you’ll remember. Whether you’re here for your morning boost,
in afternoon escape, or to discover something new, Oak & Espresso is your place to slow down
and enjoy great coffee.

Our Cafes

Oak & Espresso has outlets across multiple cities — Hyderabad, Kolkata, Delhi, Noida, Lucknow, Jaipur. Find a cafe near your location to enjoy India’s finest coffees in spaces designed meticulously to elevate your coffee experience.

Become a part of our design ideology — Modern Indian Minimalism. Our cafes are aesthetic and accented with Indian handmade accessories, yet we are rooted in practicality, sustainability, and simplicity.
